AcneBot

During my M.Eng, I opted to take courses that would help me learn to be a better engineer in terms of designing products for what people want and need. I took Advanced Mobile User Interfaces with Professor Parham Aarabi to discover more about designing a process that would suit users. It was an exploratory course that encouraged us to learn through building. Resultantly, AcneBot was born - a Facebook chat bot meant to assist individuals determine if they have acne or a condition that presents itself like acne.
