Tag'd

Tag'd was capstone project, the culmination, of my undergraduate degree in computer engineering at the University of Waterloo. I had the privelege to work on this fun project with not only peers I respected, but considered friends and roommates. The idea behind Tag'd was to create an extremely portable version of laser tag. We imaged this by creating a system that used a lot of fabric - namely fibre optic fabric.
